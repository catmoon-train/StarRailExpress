### stupid_express — Full Descriptions

**Allergist** (`allergist`)

Modifier
When player eats/drinks, one of these effects triggers randomly:
33%% nothing, 33%% immune to poison once, 33%% gain Speed 2, 1%% death.

**Monokuma** (`black_white`)

Modifier
Your class becomes Monokuma upon gaining this modifier.
See Monokuma class description for details.

**Cursed** (`cursed`)

Modifier
Gain random potion effects every 30s.
On death, this modifier passes to your killer.

**Feather** (`feather`)

Modifier
You have no collision hitbox.

**Jeb_** (`jeb_`)

Modifier
Others cannot see your name.
Every 5 seconds, you randomly change to another player's skin.

**Knight** (`knight`)

Modifier
When in darkness, chance to teleport to another player's location.

**Lovers** (`lovers`)

Modifier
You are bound to another random player as Lovers (assignment ignores factions).
If one Lover dies, the other dies of a broken heart.
If only you and your Lover survive, Lovers win.
To achieve win condition, you may kill your teammates (specifically Killer faction).
If your Lover is an Outlaw, you may not revive if they die during Outlaw Moment (as you die after them).

**Magnate** (`magnate`)

Modifier
Gain 25 coins every 60s.

**Paranoid** (`paranoid`)

Modifier
Strange sounds occasionally echo in your ears.

**Refugee** (`refugee`)

Modifier
Player with Refugee modifier revives as an Outlaw 120 seconds after death, entering Outlaw Moment.
As an Outlaw, kill all other players to win.
If killed as Outlaw, game time/inventory/coins/sanity revert to the moment of Outlaw revival.
- Note: Only above states are restored; others may be cleared/overwritten!
- Example: Arsonist fire count, Puppeteer corpse count, etc.
All players killed during Outlaw Moment revive.
During Outlaw Moment, some class/modifier active/passive abilities are disabled.
Examples:
- Deathrattle Killer corpse play/revive immunity, Necromancer revive, Avenger revenge, Admirer observation, Vulture corpse eating
- Puppeteer corpse eating, Initiate transformation, Amnesiac corpse selection, Split Personality active switch (passive still active)
- Wayfarer phase advancement (except win), Stalker phase advancement, Thief skills, Patroller passive

**Secretive** (`secretive`)

Modifier
Others cannot gain information from your corpse.

**Split Personality** (`split_personality`)

Modifier
Two souls share one body. Switch personalities every 60s or actively with key.
Default = first personality's skin.
Other personality hears and sees current personality's perspective.
When body dies, both personalities choose 'Deceive' or 'Sacrifice'.
If both choose 'Deceive', body dies instantly.
If one 'Deceive' one 'Sacrifice', 'Deceive' revives, 'Sacrifice' dies.
If both choose 'Sacrifice', both revive but live only 60s, retain inventory.
§c§lIf you die during Outlaw Moment, you will not revive.§r

**Taskmaster** (`taskmaster`)

Modifier

**Tiny** (`tiny`)

Modifier
You are shorter.

**Unyielding** (`unyielding`)

Modifier
If Civilian: Survive one accidental kill by a Civilian.
If Killer: Cannot be killed by Killer faction players.
If Neutral: Gain Stamina Boost 2.

**Vigorous** (`vigorous`)

Modifier
You have more stamina cap, stamina recovers faster when not sprinting.
